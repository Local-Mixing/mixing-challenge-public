Welcome to the local mixing project!

New to local mixing? Check out the [how-to](/howto) page. More information about the project is on the [about page](/about).

Join the discussion on [our email list](https://groups.google.com/g/local-mixing-public) or open an [issue on our Github](https://github.com/Local-Mixing/local_mixing/issues).

![a random circuit](/assets/img/ckt/front.png)

Submit your solutions in the subject line of an email to `challenges at randomcircuits dot io`. Unless otherwise specified, solutions should be written in little-endian hexadecimal in the following format:

```
Challenge ##: 0x5819bae...
```

We will post new challenges occasionally. There is currently no reward for any challenges.
